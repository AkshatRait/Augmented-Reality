Quality of Life

There are many things that Augmented Reality may be able to do. One important thing that AR can do is that it may be able to help improve a person's quality of life. Here are some ways AR can do that.

Displaying Objects

With Augmented Reality, we are able to display virtual objects into the real world. With this functionality, we are able to display objects into your space. There are endless uses with this feature such as planning where you put your furniture and placing creative decorations.

Mapping your Space

Not sure where you placed your item? One way AR may be able to help that is by keeping track of where certain items are. This will improve organization and storage use. This can be extremely useful in places such as retail.

Navigation

Being able to use a map to get to places is an necessities in a person's life. If you are unfamiliar with your surroundings or do not know how to get to your destination, you would need a map to help you navigate.

Current Technology

With our smartphones, we can use navigation apps, such as Google Maps, to help us navigate the streets.

Lead the Way

With AR, navigation can be even easier as it can help navigate you on where to go in the real world. Virtual arrows can guide you to your destination.
